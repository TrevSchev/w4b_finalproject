About Me

Objective: I am a graphic designer raised in the San Francisco Bay Area and trained in the graphic design program at California State University East Bay. Though I may not be a storyteller myself, one thing I strive to do is capture the feelings of stories being told whether that be a story coming from real life interaction, such as a taekwondo instructor and his students, or from music, books, movies, and video games. As a fan of all of these forms of storytelling I often find inspiration in their creativity and depth which makes me strive to always want to be better. It is my ultimate goal to one day help a movie or video game studio bring their creation to life. Awards: In High School I won an anti smoking poster contest put on by the School Wellness and Safety Program by the Alameda County Office of Education because of my use of imagery and text in order to convey the message. This first success helped spark my creative journey in order to learn more about art digitally and physically so that I could begin to grow and learn to create work that is entirely my own.
